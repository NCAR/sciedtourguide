Visitor Information The NCAR Visitor Center is located at the NCAR Mesa Lab in Boulder, Colorado. Admission is always free. For more information, please view our visit page. Address 1850 Table Mesa Drive
Boulder, Colorado 80305

Visitor Center Hours Weekdays: 8:00 am - 5:00 pm
Weekends: 9:00 am - 4:00 pm

The visitor center is open every day except for two annual shut down days in the spring and fall. Check our visit page for updates when these shut downs are scheduled.
